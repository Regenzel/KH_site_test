About the Saga

Kingdom Hearts is an action role-playing game series that blends original characters with iconic worlds from Disney and Square Enix. The story follows Sora, a young boy chosen to wield the mysterious Keyblade, a weapon tied to the balance between light and darkness.
Across the saga, players explore a vast interconnected universe made up of many different worlds, each with its own identity, characters, and challenges. Alongside his companions Donald Duck and Goofy, Sora embarks on a journey that tests friendship, identity, and the meaning of the heart.
At its core, Kingdom Hearts is a tale about connections: between people, worlds, and memories. The series is known for its emotional storytelling, fast-paced combat, and deep lore that unfolds over multiple games, timelines, and perspectives.
While accessible on the surface, the narrative gradually reveals greater complexity as the journey continues. Combining action gameplay, memorable music, and a unique crossover concept, Kingdom Hearts has become a long-running and beloved franchise celebrated for its heart, ambition, and imagination.
